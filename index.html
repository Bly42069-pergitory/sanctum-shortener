Our Method

Stone · Memory · Permanence

Three disciplines. One standard: surfaces restored with integrity, documented with care, and protected for the long horizon.

I

Assessment

We read the stone first — substrate, veining, prior repairs, and environmental exposure — before any tool meets the surface.

II

Restoration

Controlled honing, polish, crack stabilization, and color-matched infill — executed by hand for surfaces meant to outlast trends.

III

Protection

Impregnating sealers, maintenance protocols, and conservation-minded guidance so the work holds its finish year after year.
